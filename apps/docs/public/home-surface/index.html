Portal Surface

home.iai.one

home.iai.one is the Cloudflare Pages portal for the IAI ecosystem. In the live snapshot on March 28, 2026, it responds with 200 HTML and serves as the practical gateway from public narrative into product surfaces.

Portal Purpose

Orient

Explain the system map fast enough that a new visitor knows where to go next.

Route

Direct the right audiences into App, Flow, Docs, Dash, and Developer without forcing them to guess.

Filter

Separate vision and product entry from detailed operational or technical documentation.

What The Portal Must Always Link

  • App Surface and the live app.iai.one entry.
  • Flow System and the builder entry.
  • Developer Portal for technical onboarding.
  • Service Registry for the full domain map.
  • Security and Deployment for teams evaluating implementation depth.

How It Differs From iai.one

Simple rule

iai.one is the constitutional root. home.iai.one is the routing and orientation portal. If both try to do the same job, the system ends up with two weak homepages instead of a clean entry path.
